Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson, a delagate from Virginia, was the principal author of the Declaration of Independence. He later served as the 3rd President of the United States and is remembered for his contributions to American ideals of liberty and democracy.
John Adams
John Adams, representing Massachusetts, was a vocal advocate for independence in the Continental Congress. He became the 2nd President of the United States and played a key role in drafting foundational documents and promoting the cause of freedom.
